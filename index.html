About Us

Bringing Ocean Science and Space Exploration into the Classroom

Our Mission: Bridging Science and Education

PACE in Classroom is an innovative project aimed at bridging the gap between complex scientific data and students worldwide. Our mission is to enhance ocean literacy by presenting the data collected by NASA’s PACE (Plankton, Aerosol, Cloud, ocean Ecosystem) satellite in a way that is engaging, digestible, and easy to understand. We believe that by making this crucial information accessible, we can inspire the next generation to care about and actively contribute to preserving our planet's oceans.

Meet the Team Driving Ocean Literacy

Our team consists of passionate individuals committed to fostering a deeper understanding of the Earth’s oceans and the role they play in global climate systems. Through our interactive platform, students can explore real-time data, gain insights into ocean ecosystems, and develop an appreciation for the environment.
